When my hands touch the key board, and I turned on my computer screen, It was another world. I went from restaurant manager to software developer, from service to development. I have learned from so many and my passion to continue learning never stops. I plan on bringing Memphis my love for tech, my love for problem solving, and my skills ive gained on the way of my journey to this day.
